---
title: "DeepSeek"
description: "DeepSeek-family models in the Compose catalog."
---

DeepSeek-family names can be served by different upstream providers. Compose does not special-case them by name.

Use catalog search:

```http
POST /v1/models/search
```

Then call the returned public `modelId`. The catalog row decides the provider and upstream model ID.
