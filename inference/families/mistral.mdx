---
title: "Mistral"
description: "Mistral-family models in the Compose catalog."
---

Mistral-family model names can appear through different providers. Compose does not route them through a hardcoded Mistral bucket.

Search the generated catalog and use the returned public ID:

```http
POST /v1/models/search
```

The selected catalog row determines the provider and adapter.
