description: "Black Forest Labs and FLUX-family image rows."
---

FLUX-family image rows can appear through providers such as Cloudflare, Vertex, or other catalog sources. Compose does not route by the family name alone.

Search the catalog for `flux` and use the returned public ID:

```http
POST /v1/models/search
```

For image generation, call:

```http
POST /v1/images/generations
```
